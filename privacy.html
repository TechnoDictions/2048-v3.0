Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 2026


Overview:
This Privacy Policy describes how 2048 Game Blazas ("we," "our," or "the Game") handles your information when you use our application.
1. Information We Collect: Google Account Data: If you sign in via Google, we collect your name and profile picture URL. Game Data: We store your high scores and game progress in our database (powered by Supabase). Local Storage: We use browser Local Storage to remember your player name for a better experience.
2. How We Use Information: To display your name and avatar on the leaderboard. To save and sync your high scores across different sessions.
3. Data Sharing: We do not sell your personal data. Your name and score are visible to other players on the global leaderboard.
4. Data Deletion: Users can request data deletion by contacting us at blazasinfo@gmail.com or by clicking the "Logout" and "Clear Data" options within the game.
